# Kampus Filter

## Overview

Kampus Filter is a daily platform that helps students stay updated with the information that matters most.

Every day, students get a short, easy-to-read briefing with important education updates, opportunities, and career insights—all in about five minutes.

Instead of searching through multiple websites and social media, students receive only the most useful information in one place.

Every update answers three simple questions:

1. What happened?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What should I do next?

Kampus Filter is built for Class 11 and 12 students, drop-year aspirants, and competitive exam students who want to make better education and career decisions.

The daily briefing covers three areas:

### Education

Exam updates, admissions, scholarships, universities, and important announcements.

### Opportunities

Internships, competitions, fellowships, ambassador programs, scholarships, and other student opportunities.

### Career

Career trends, future skills, emerging jobs, salaries, and industry insights.

## Mission

**Help students cut through the noise and make better decisions about their future.**
